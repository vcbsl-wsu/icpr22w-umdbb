Paper submission Instructions

Paper submissions to UMDBB 2022 are limited to 6 pages plus unlimited pages for references in the IEEE format. All the contributing authors are required to mention their names and affiliations following the main ICPR conference single-blind review policy. The submitted papers will be reviewed by at least two technical program committee members identified as an expert in the field. Papers will be selected based on relevance, significance and novelty of results, technical merit, and clarity of presentation. Accepted papers will be included in the ICPR 2022 Workshop Proceedings, which will be published according to the publication rules of the ICPR conference. The papers should be submitted using the Microsoft Conference Management Toolkit (CMT) site given below.
